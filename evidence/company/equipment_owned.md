# Equipment Owned

Archaeo-Consult Ltd. maintains a comprehensive inventory of in-house equipment, ensuring efficient and reliable project delivery. Our equipment includes:

*   **Survey Equipment:** Total Stations (Leica TS16), GNSS RTK GPS systems (Trimble R10), drone for aerial photography and photogrammetry.
*   **Geophysical Survey Equipment:** Magnetometers (Geoscan Research FM256 Fluxgate Gradiometer), resistivity meters (Geoscan Research RM85).
*   **Vehicles:** A fleet of 4x4 vehicles suitable for all-terrain site access.
*   **Excavation Kit:** Full range of hand tools, sieving equipment, site fencing, welfare units, and safety equipment.

All equipment is regularly maintained and calibrated to industry standards.
