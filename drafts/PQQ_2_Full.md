### Q: C3.1 Mandatory exclusion grounds?

Archaeo-Consult Ltd. confirms that neither the organisation nor any director, partner, or any other person with powers of representation, decision, or control has been convicted of any of the offences listed under Regulation 57(1) of the Public Contracts Regulations 2015, which include, but are not limited to, conspiracy, corruption, bribery, fraud, and terrorist offences. A signed declaration can be provided upon request.

### Q: C3.2 Discretionary exclusion grounds?

Archaeo-Consult Ltd. confirms the following:
*   The organisation has not been convicted of any criminal offence relating to the conduct of its business or profession.
*   The organisation has not committed any act of grave professional misconduct in the course of its business or profession.
*   The organisation is fully compliant with all its obligations relating to the payment of social security contributions and taxes under the law of the United Kingdom.

### Q: C5.1 ISO 14001 or equivalent?

While not formally ISO 14001 certified at this time, Archaeo-Consult Ltd. operates an internal Environmental Management System (EMS) that is aligned with the principles and objectives of the ISO 14001:2015 standard. Our system is regularly reviewed to ensure we effectively manage our environmental aspects and impacts.
